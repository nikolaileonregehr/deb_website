Global education made possible through the internet.

DEB is a 100% remote NGO focusing on education for children and teenagers in developing countries. Our teachers are experienced in educating teenagers over the internet.

Our mission

Creating opportunities

Digital Education Bridge leverages the internet to overcome local education gaps in developing countries. Our experienced teachers give weekly remote classes to small batches of promising students. We are a 100% remote NGO dedicated to reduce poverty by creating opportunities through education.

Our vision

Education for a better life

We believe in the power of education to improve one's life. Offering free education to motivated teenagers allows us to intervene positively in the most crucial part of their lifes. We dream of enabling thousands of alumni to study in another country, learn professional skills with high market value and create a better life for their families and countries.
